About me

I’m an expat Canadian and PhD candidate in statistics at The University of Auckland in New Zealand. Prior to moving down under, I was a senior research analyst with the Government of Newfoundland & Labrador in St. John’s, Canada.

I’m interested in things like machine learning, functional programming, probability, and computer science; many of the elements of very applied math, which I enjoy attempting to grok.
